Rice Eats

About

Rice Eats is a web app designed for students at Rice University to view the meals currently availible at each of the serveries, and then leave reviews for those meals and view the rating of each meal as determined by the community of students.

To leave a review just click or tap on one of the 5 grey circles next to the item you wish to review.
